    -https://ieeexplore-ieee-org.proxy.lib.pdx.edu/stamp/stamp.jsp?tp=&arnumber=850652
This paper introduces a coevolutionary method developed for solving constrained optimization problems.
This algorithm is based on the evolution of two populations with opposite objectives to solve saddle-point problems.
